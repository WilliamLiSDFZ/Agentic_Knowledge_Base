# Light Field Microscopy Image Reconstruction

This skill covers computational methods for reconstructing 3D scenes and biological samples from specialized imaging data, primarily using light field microscopy, Gaussian splatting, and diffusion models. Specific tasks include 3D localization, virtual staining, phase imaging, and the enhancement or restoration of images from spike cameras, monocular video, and low-light or rainy conditions.
