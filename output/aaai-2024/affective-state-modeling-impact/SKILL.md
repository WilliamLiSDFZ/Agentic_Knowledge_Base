This skill involves developing and applying computational models to detect, recognize, and analyze human affective states (e.g., emotions, depression) from behavioral and physiological data. Core methods include domain generalization, data augmentation, and multimodal fusion, specifically applied to tasks like EEG-based emotion recognition, cognitive diagnosis with affect, and depression detection from video or text data.
