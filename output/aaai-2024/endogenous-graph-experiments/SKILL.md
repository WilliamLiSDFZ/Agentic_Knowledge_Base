# Endogenous Graph Experiments

This skill involves designing and analyzing controlled experiments on networks where the connections between units (the graph) are not externally fixed but can be influenced by the treatments or behaviors of the units themselves. It focuses on methods to address interference and network formation, with applications in testing social contagion, peer effects, and platform policies in social networks and marketplaces.
