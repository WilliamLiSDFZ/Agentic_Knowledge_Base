## Abstract

AbstractThis tutorial provides an overview of the cutting edge research on spatial and temporal language understanding. We also cover some essential background material from various subdisciplines to this topic, which we believe will enrich the CL community’s appreciation of the complexity of spatiotemporal reasoning.
