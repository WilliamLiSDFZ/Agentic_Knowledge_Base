# Speaker Noise Invariant Acoustic Learning

This skill covers methods for learning robust acoustic representations that are invariant to speaker characteristics and background noise, primarily using self-supervised learning with acoustic units or "pieces" as targets. The core task is to pre-train models on large, unlabeled audio to perform well on downstream applications like speech recognition and speaker verification in noisy, multi-speaker environments.
