This skill covers methods for adapting large language models (LLMs) to zero-shot and multimodal tasks without task-specific training, including techniques like self-improving prompting, speculative decoding, and cross-modal alignment distillation. It specifically addresses applications such as named entity recognition, speech understanding, multimodal summarization, image classification, and document translation by leveraging model prompting, knowledge amalgamation, and explainable bottlenecks.
