# Human Judgment Embedding Adjustment

This skill involves using human feedback to refine and align the semantic dimensions of vector embeddings, employing methods like direct assessment and pairwise comparisons to adjust interpretable features. Its applications include improving the controllability and fairness of language models and enhancing retrieval systems by grounding abstract vector spaces in human conceptual judgments.
