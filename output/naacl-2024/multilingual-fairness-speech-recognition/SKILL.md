This skill covers evaluating and mitigating demographic and linguistic bias in automatic speech recognition (ASR) and audio models, using methods like fairness metrics, federated learning, and pre-trained model analysis. Its core tasks are developing equitable multilingual systems for applications like ASR and hate speech detection, with a focus on low-resource languages, disfluent speech, and deepfake detection.
