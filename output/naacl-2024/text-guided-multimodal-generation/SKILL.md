# Text Guided Multimodal Generation

This skill covers methods for aligning and generating across modalities—like speech, images, music, and video—using language models and diffusion models as central controllers or editors. Specific tasks include controllable synthesis (e.g., text-to-music, aspect-controlled referring expressions), training-free composition, and inference-time collaboration between models for applications in captioning, editing, and multimodal dialogue.
