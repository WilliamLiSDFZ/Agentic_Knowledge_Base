# Narrative Endpoint Generation

This skill involves generating coherent narrative storylines that begin and end at thematically or situationally connected points, often using methods like cyclic plot generation, constraint-based story planning, and neural language models fine-tuned on paired narrative endpoints. Its primary tasks include creating satisfyingly closed story arcs, exploratory storytelling, and applications in interactive fiction, automated story generation, and creative writing assistance.
